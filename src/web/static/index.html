Key Concepts

Stop-LossAuto-sells if price drops 2% below entry
Take-ProfitAuto-sells when price rises 4% above entry
Trailing StopFollows price up, sells if it drops 1.5% from peak
Kill SwitchEmergency halt — stops ALL trading instantly
Market RegimeBULL/BEAR/VOLATILE/SIDEWAYS — AI adjusts per regime
Scanner Score0-100 opportunity ranking (higher = better)
Sharpe RatioRisk-adjusted return. >1 good, >2 excellent
Profit FactorTotal wins / total losses. >1 = profitable
